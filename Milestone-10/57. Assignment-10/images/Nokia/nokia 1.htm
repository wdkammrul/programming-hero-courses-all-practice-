    Step up to smarter

    Nokia 1

    Step up to your first smartphone. Enjoy all of the apps you need to connect with friends and family, stay entertained and find your way around town. Capture the moment day or night thanks to the rear camera with LED flash, then share the shot using 4G connectivity.

    Software

    Enjoy a smooth, streamlined experience with Android 10 (Go edition)

    Hardware

    Featuring a quad-core processor for optimized performance

    Design

    Combining an iconic look with reliable durability

    Cameras

    5 MP rear camera and a 2 MP front camera

    Apps

    All the apps you need

    Nokia 1 doesn’t hold back. A powerful experience in our most affordable package, with all your favorite apps like WhatsApp, Facebook, YouTube, Google Maps and many more, literally at your fingertips. You won’t miss a beat.

    Entertainment

    Watch now – and later

    Nokia 1 comes with YouTube Go, designed to run faster and use less data while you view videos and stream. You can choose the playback quality and even download videos to watch offline later.

    Camera

    Cherish the moment

    Relive those special moments and stay connected. Capture the world around you with the 5 MP rear camera with LED flash or share selfies and make video calls with the 2 MP front camera.

    Design

    Your next phone

    Nokia 1 offers an optimized smartphone experience, accessible to everyone. Its design details give it the iconic look you love and the durability you need. And the exchangeable Xpress-on covers are back too. Some things are timeless.

    Accessories

    Make it yours

    Personalize your phone to match your style or mood. Xpress-on covers are quick and easy to swap out and come in a variety of vibrant colors to choose from.

    Hardware

    Get more done

    Smooth to use, Nokia 1 is full of possibilities. Enjoy 4G LTE connectivity for streaming and surfing, and easily switch between SIM cards thanks to Dual SIM. Or take advantage of the MicroSD storage card slot to expand storage up to 128 GB1.

    Software

    Android™ 10 (Go edition) is here

    Android 10 (Go edition) delivers the latest Android experience with optimized apps like Google Assistant Go, designed to use less storage space.

    Save data

    Worry less about wasting data with the Chrome Data Saver. You’ll save up to 60% of mobile data.

    You ask, it answers

    Nokia 1 comes with Google Assistant Go edition⁵. Ask questions, tell it to do things, whatever you like – just say the word.

    Pure, secure and up to date

    Bringing Android 10 (Go edition) to Nokia 1 is just part of our software upgrade promise.
